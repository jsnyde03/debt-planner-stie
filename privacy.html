Privacy Policy for Paycheck Debt Planner Effective Date: June 2026 Paycheck Debt Planner respects your privacy. DATA COLLECTION Debt Planner does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal financial information on external servers. All budgeting, debt, and payment information entered into the app is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export or back up your data yourself. Limited diagnostic data may be collected by RevenueCat solely for subscription management purposes.
